Nasa posts an "Image of the Day" page every day.  Here are 3 of my favourite images;
 

Voyager 1s Mission to the Outer Planet Begins
"the Voyager mission was designed to take advantage of a rare geometric arrangement of the outer planets in the late 1970s and the 1980s which allowed for a four-planet tour for a minimum of propellant and trip time. " - NASA
 

IXPE Measures Exploded Star Remains
"When a massive star collapsed in the Cassiopeia constellation, it generated a supernova explosion with some of the fastest shockwaves in the Milky Way." - NASA
 

Hubble Snaps a Pair of Interacting Galaxies
"The two interacting galaxies making up the pair known as Arp-Madore 608-333 seem to float side by side in this image from the NASA/ESA Hubble space Telescope." - NASA
